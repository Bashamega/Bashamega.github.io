Debate: No Guns

My teacher and I debated about guns. I was against guns, and he was for guns.
We argued about six points:

1. More than 1,000 school children died in school shootings since 1970.
2. If people have guns, they can protect themselves from burglars or other criminals.
3. Each day twelve children die from gun violence FOR NO REASON, and another thirty-five children are shot and injured in America.
4. People need guns to go hunting for food, like deer or bears. Should those people starve because they can’t hunt any food?
5. Some people fire a gun at their wedding in Egypt and some can die or get injured.
6. Gun factories create many jobs for people. If we close gun factories, many people will lose their jobs and have no money.
After the debate I won five of the debates and he won one debate.
The debate was hot, like fire.

Debate Arguments

1. More than 1,000 school children died in school shootings since 1970.

  • School shootings don’t happen because of guns. They happen because people aren’t being raised to learn that violence is wrong. Parents should teach their children that violence is wrong.
  • It is not the parents’ fault, because they could be unaware or travelling around the world and not taking care of their children.
  • It is the parents’ fault because every parent should know if his or her child has access to guns. If the parent is too busy or unaware, they should be punished if their child commits a crime.
  • How can they know if their child keeps the weapon (gun, etc.) secretly outside the house and school, like at the park or in the vents of the apartment?

2. If people have guns, they can protect themselves from burglars or other criminals.

  • I think you’re correct, but in many countries the law says that you can’t carry a gun to defend yourself or to kill or injure someone.
  • It is true that owning guns is illegal in many countries, but in the USA, it is important for people to be able to protect their families. If they don’t have guns, they might get robbed or injured by a criminal who has a gun.
  • Yes, it is true and that means we don’t know if guns are good or bad. But let’s jump back to argument number one, some kids do these things because their parents own a gun in their house.
  • If parents have a gun in the house, they should be certain to teach their kids that it is dangerous and that they shouldn’t use it.
  • Nearly half of all parents with a weapon in the home wrongly believe their children don’t know where they hid it, but if they find it they could kill someone, even their parents if they are angry with them.

3. Each day 12 children die from gun violence FOR NO REASON, and another thirty-five children are shot and injured in America.

  • If kids live in a dangerous neighborhood, they should only play in safer areas and always go with their parents or a guardian for safety.
  • What if the place you are talking about is a school, and parents can’t afford to homeschool their kids at home, and can’t go to school with them? Do you say that more than one hundred kids should drop out of school?
  • Once again, this violence is happening because there are no police or teachers watching the students play place. Police and teachers, or parents, should always watch the kids.
  • You mean like a bodyguard? The world doesn’t have enough bodyguards for all these kids around the world.

4. People need guns to go hunting for food, like deer or bears. Should those people starve because they can’t hunt any food?

  • I didn’t mean all guns. Hunters can go hunt at any time, but before they return to their homes, they should return the gun to the pawn shop or the place they rented it from.
  • Many people who need to hunt for food live in the forest, or far away from any city or town. So, they can’t go to a pawn shop, or store whenever they need to hunt.
  • They can buy one, but they must return it or leave it in their house while they’re in town, the city, or at the mall.

5. Some people fire a gun at their wedding in Egypt and some can die or get injured.

  • If people shoot guns at weddings, that means it is a tradition and it is important to them and their family. So, if someone gets injured, they should just be more careful and shoot away from the crowd.
  • But the shot can injure someone or kill someone two miles away. Like there was a teenage girl on the roof and in one second, she was dead on the ground, and another story says that someone was watching the wedding and got shot in her eye, and she is now blind.
  • If guns are dangerous at weddings, just ban them at weddings. It doesn’t mean that people can’t own guns, they just can’t bring them to weddings.
  • But these accidents can happen at any time and anywhere. There is a story that a kid younger than me took a gun to play with it and killed his friends, and another story says while a father was cleaning the gun he shot himself in his leg and died.

الهنود الحمر يعرفون أيضًا باسم الأمريكيون الأصليون. الهنود الأمريكيون يطلقون عليهم هذا الاسم من قبل كريستوفر كولومبس. الهنود الحمر هم السكان الأصليون للأمريكيتان قبل عصر كريستوفر كولمبس، سُمُّوا بالهنود الحمر؛ لأن كريستوفر كولومبس ظن خطأ أنه في الهند عندما اكتشفها. على مر آلاف الأعوام، ظل الهنود الحمر يزرعون العديد من المحاصيل. وتمثل هذه النباتات 50-60% من مجمل المحاصيل في العالم. وفي بعض الحالات، قام السكان الأصليون بتطوير وتهجين أنواع وسلالات مستحدثة من النباتات عن طريق الاصطفاء الاصطناعي، وقد احتفظ العديد من هذه المحاصيل الزراعية بأسمائها المأخوذة من لغة السكان الأصليين في اللغتين الإنجليزية والإسبانية. ينقسم الهنود الحمر من حيث الدين إلى مسيحيين ووثنيين (معتقدات وممارسات تتفق على عبادة الطبيعة.)، ويعزى سبب انتشار الديانة المسيحية بينهم إلى نشاطات التبشير واحتكاكهم بالمستوطنين الأمريكيين البيض.

The six kingdoms of life

Even though there are over 8 million different species in the world, they can all be classified into only six different general classes that share similar traits. It is an amazing fact that connects all life together. Before microscopes were discovered biologists only thought there were two classes of life – plants and animals.

The six kingdoms of life are:

Archaebacteria – Single-celled organisms often found in extreme conditions, like thermal vents, or hot boiling water.

Eubacteria – Similar to Archaebacteria, but more complex with multiple cells connected together. Most bacteria are in this class.

Protists – Microscopic organisms that are not bacteria, not animals, not plants and not fungi with more complex cells. Algae and slime mold are good example of a protist.

Fungi – Fungi are multicellular and consist of many complex cells. Mushrooms are members of this group. They are similar to plants, but cannot make their own food.

Plantae – Plantae are all multicellular and consist of complex cells. They can be a tiny as moss and as big as a giant tree. They usually can make their own food.

Animalia – This is the largest kingdom. Humans are part of it as well as millions of other species that include everything from tigers to insects. An important characteristic are very complex cells and the need to get energy (food) from outside sources (known as heterotrophs).

شخصٌ يقود سيارتهِ في مدينةٍ ساحليةٍ، وأراد القيام بنزهةٍ بحرية, دون نزول من سيارتهِ, فاتجه إلى الماء, وضغطَ على زرٍ, فتتحوّل السيارةُ إلى القاربَ, ليتنزه به! هذه ليست سيارةً عادية، ولكنَّها سيارةٌ مبتكرةٌ اسمها (السيارةُ البرمائية). سميت هكذا لأن يمكن أن تسير فوق اليابس والمياه. بداية صنع السيارة البرمائية: أجريت العديدُ من التجاربِ للتوصلِ إلى السيارةِ، ولكنها فشلت لعدمِ قدرة الفنيين على زيادةِ سرعةِ في الماءِ عن عشرةِ كيلومتراتٍ في الساعةِ، وهي بطيئةٌ، يمكن لسباحٍ تجاوزها إذا كانت بجانبه! السيارة الجديدة التي صنعت في انجلترا، وسرعته على البحر أسرع, كما تصلُ سرعتها في الماءِ إلى خمسين كيلومترًا في الساعة وتستطيع أن تمخرَ عباب الماء, ومن ثم ستكونُ السيارةً رياضيةً, توفر نزهاتٍ بريةً رائعةً لمن يقتنيها. تم تجربة السيارة في نهرِ التايمز، ونجحت، وكما يقول عنها مديرُ الشركة التي تنتجها: إنها سيارة فريدة, لأنها مصممةٌ من البدايةِ على أن تكون برمائية, فهي إذن ليست قاربًا له عجلات أو سيارة يمكنُها فقط الصمودُ في الماءِ! كيفية عمل هذه السيارة: للسيارةِ ثلاثة مقاعدٍ، وهيكلُها مصنوعٌ من ألياف الكربون لتكون خفيف وقادرة على التحمل, وكذلك هي مزودةٌ بمحركاتٍ ذاتِ قوة دفعٍ كبيرةٍ حتى تجعلَ جسمها يبقى طافيًا بالسطح, ولا يغطسُ. وتعمل هذه المحركات داخل الماء بواسطة مضخاتٍ ونفاثات الماء, كما تعمل الزلاّجات التي تراها وهي تنطلقُ بسرعةٍ بالقرب من الشاطئ, وتصدر عنها نافورات من الماء. ولكن مضخات ونفاثات السيارة البرمائية, أكبر وأشدُ, حتى من تلك الموجودة في الزوارق البخاريةِ السريعةِ. السيارةُ مزودة بكمبيوترِ يتحكم في معظمِ عملياتها، فهو يقومُ برفعِ ثلاثةِ إطارات في مدةٍ قصيرة, عند النزول إلى الماءِ, ووضعها بصفةٍ مؤقتةٍ بأماكنَ محددة في أسفلِ هيكلِ السيارة, لحين إعادتها إلى مكانها الطبيعي بمجرد الخروجِ من الماء. أما الإطارُ الرابع للسيارةِ, فإنه يبقى مكانه للوقايةِ من النفاثات القوية للمياهِ. وتُغطى الأجزاءُ السفلية من السيارةِ بإحكام حتى لا تتخللها المياه, حتى لو اندفعت السيارةُ بسرعتها القصوى على الماء.

Hatshepsut

Hatshepsut was an Ancient-Egyptian ruler was the longest-ruling and most successful female pharaoh. She was born into royalty around 1507BCE. Her father and her brothers were pharaohs, which made her a princess. But she became a powerful ruler. Hatshepsut began in 1478 BCE when she married Pharaoh Thutmose ll at age 12. When her husband died, she ruled in place of his son. At some point, however, Hatshepsut proclaimed herself pharaoh rather than continuing to act as the boy’s regent until he was old enough to rule. Many Egyptians referred to Hatshepsut as a king, not a queen. As a ruler, Hatshepsut worked with other kingdoms to create relationships, and she opened new trade routes. Egypt was a wealthy and peaceful place during her rule. Hatshepsut also dedicates her reign to building magnificent temples and monuments, to help poor people. Her most impressive building was her mortuary temple, a burial place for all of Egypt’s most powerful pharaohs. This temple became the standard for all future pharaohs. Many of Hatshepsut’s buildings are now recognized as architectural marvels. Hatshepsut died at a young age, and many believe she was poised. Following her death, pharaohs attempted to erase her from Egyptian history, possible to hide the fact that a woman had been a pharaoh. And they were almost successful. Her reign was unknown to scholars for many centuries until researchers began to dig deeper in to ancient texts. Which pointed a powerful woman ruler. Today, you can find Hatshepsut’s artifacts in nearly every major museum.
